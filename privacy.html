Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Keepy ("the App") is a mobile application designed to help users track and manage their subscriptions. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Keepy does not collect, store, or process any personal data on external servers.

All subscription data entered by users (such as subscription name, price, billing date, and category) is stored locally on the user's device.

2. Personal Data

The App does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or location.

3. How Data Is Used

User data is used solely to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Displaying subscription lists
  • Generating spending statistics
  • Sending payment reminders

4. Data Sharing

Keepy does not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use operating system notification services to deliver reminders. These services operate under their own privacy policies.
